## PSO-16C-001

Date: 2026-06-17

Origin:
Engine v0.1

Related Phase:
16.C — Discoverability

Status:
Observed

### Observation

Existence does not imply Discoverability.

Discoverability does not imply existence.

An EON may infer the existence of a non-existent path.

An EON may fail to infer the existence of an existing path.

### Conclusion

exists_path()

and

can_discover()

are independent functions.

### Impact

This observation does not modify the sealed definition of Discoverability.

It clarifies the distinction between:

* structural reality
* epistemic accessibility

preserved by Engine v0.1 tests.
